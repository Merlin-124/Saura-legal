Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026 · Version 1.0

Saura ("we," "our," "us") is a fitness and social training app. We take your privacy seriously — especially because the app involves body measurements, training history, and personal photos. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, who we share it with, and what control you have over your data.

By using Saura, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please don't use the app.

1. Who we are

Saura is operated by Merlin Padilla. We are the data controller for all personal data described in this policy. You can reach us at saura.app.help@gmail.com for any privacy question, request, or complaint.

2. Information we collect

Account information

  • Email address (required to sign up and sign in).
  • Password (stored only as a one-way hash by our authentication provider — we never see your plaintext password).
  • Display name and avatar (if you provide them).
  • Date of birth (collected once at sign-up only to verify you meet our minimum age of 16). It is stored with your account record and is never shown to other users.

Health and body information

  • Body weight, height, sex, and training goal (if you provide them during onboarding or in Settings).
  • Workout logs: exercises, sets, reps, weights, dates.
  • Nutrition logs: calories, macronutrients, water intake (if you choose to log them).
  • Recovery and activity data: sleep duration, resting heart rate, heart-rate variability (HRV), steps, and estimated calories burned (if you choose to log them or connect a wearable).
  • Progress photos (if you choose to upload them). Progress photos are stored in a private bucket and visible only to you; they are never shown to other users or in the social feed.

Social-feature information

  • Posts you create (caption, photos or videos, attached workout).
  • Likes, comments, and follows you create (visible to other users through the normal social-feed mechanics).
  • Blocks you create (visible only to you — the blocked user is never notified).
  • Reports you file against other users or posts (visible only to you and to our moderation review).

Device and technical information

  • Push notification token (required to deliver notifications). Stored only while you remain signed in on that device.
  • IP address at sign-in (logged automatically by our authentication provider for security/fraud detection).

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your precise location (no GPS access).
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not show third-party advertising and we do not share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not use your training data to train external AI models.

3. How we use your information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide the core features of the app — log workouts, compute analytics, display recovery scores, generate your goal-aligned leaderboard, render the social feed.
  • Deliver push notifications you've enabled (likes, comments, friend PRs).
  • Personalize the experience to your training goal and preferences.
  • Detect and prevent abuse, spam, and harassment.

4. Who we share it with

Service providers (data processors)

We use trusted third parties to run the service. They process data only on our behalf:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, file storage, and edge functions. Stores all of the information described in section 2. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Expo — delivers push notifications via Apple's push notification service. Receives only the push token and the notification payload. The payload contains the notifying user's display name and, for comments, a short sanitized preview of the comment text. Expo Privacy Policy.
  • Apple — receives the notification payload (including the notifying user's display name and, for comments, a short comment preview) to deliver pushes to your device. Apple's data practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.

Other Saura users

If you post to the feed, follow another user, or comment on a post, your display name, avatar, and that activity are visible to other authenticated users of Saura. When you attach a workout to a post, that workout's exercises, sets, reps, and weights become visible to anyone who can view the post. Your sensitive data (weight, height, sex, recovery readings, progress photos) is never visible to other users — it is protected by our database access controls and a private storage bucket.

Legal requests

We may disclose information when required by law (subpoena, court order) or to protect the safety of users or the public. We will push back on overbroad requests.

5. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account from inside the app:

  • Your authentication record is permanently removed.
  • Your workouts, posts, comments, likes, follows, push tokens, recovery readings, nutrition logs, and progress photos are permanently deleted (cascade delete).
  • Your avatar, post images, and progress photo files are deleted from storage.

Some operational logs (sign-in IPs, server access logs) are retained by our infrastructure provider for up to 90 days for security and abuse-prevention purposes (legitimate interest under GDPR Art. 6(1)(f) where applicable), after which they are automatically purged.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you have one or more of the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — fix inaccurate or outdated data directly in the app or by contacting us.
  • Deletion — delete your account in Settings, or contact us. This permanently removes the data described in section 5.
  • Objection / restriction — limit or object to certain processing of your data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format. Contact us to request an export.

To exercise any of these rights, email saura.app.help@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

Legal basis (EU/UK/EEA residents)

This section applies if you are a resident of the EU, UK, or EEA. We process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) — to provide the core app features you've signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) — to detect and prevent abuse, spam, and harassment, and to keep the service secure.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — for push notifications and progress photos, which you can withdraw at any time in Settings.

Right to complain. If you believe we have mishandled your data, you may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. A list is maintained by the European Data Protection Board at edpb.europa.eu.

Providing data. Providing your email and password is required to create an account; everything else (display name, height, weight, photos, etc.) is optional. We do not use automated decision-making that produces legal effects.

7. California Privacy Rights

This section applies if you are a California resident. The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) give you the following rights:

  • Right to know what categories of personal information we collect, the sources, the business purposes, and the third parties we share it with — all described in sections 2 and 4 of this policy.
  • Right to delete your personal information — exercise this by deleting your account in Settings or by emailing us.
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to opt out of sale or sharing. Saura does not sell or share your personal information, as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA. We have not sold or shared personal information in the preceding twelve months.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising any of the above rights.

Submit any California rights request to the contact address in section 12; we verify identity by confirming control of the account email and respond within 45 days.

Categories of personal information collected (CCPA taxonomy): identifiers (email, user ID), customer records (name, avatar, date of birth), internet/network activity (sign-in IP), geolocation (none), biometric (none), sensory (photos you upload), professional (none), education (none), inferences (training analytics computed from your workouts).

Categories of sources: directly from you.

Business purposes: providing the app, security and fraud prevention, communicating with you.

Third parties with whom information is shared: Supabase and Expo, as service providers under written agreements that prohibit use of your data for any purpose other than running Saura on our behalf.

8. Children's privacy

Saura is intended for users aged 16 and older. We enforce this with a date-of-birth check at sign-up, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If we learn that we have collected data from a user under 16, we will delete that data and terminate the account.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided data to us, please contact saura.app.help@gmail.com and we will act promptly.

9. Security

We use the following safeguards to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Row-level security policies that scope every database query to the authenticated user.
  • A private storage bucket for progress photos, accessed only via short-lived signed URLs.
  • One-way password hashing — we never store plaintext passwords.
  • Mandatory email confirmation at sign-up.

No system is perfectly secure. If a breach occurs that materially affects you, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by law.

10. International data transfers

Saura is operated from the United States. Our infrastructure providers (Supabase, Expo) may process data in the United States and other jurisdictions. For transfers of personal data out of the EEA, these providers rely on Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission. See Supabase's and Expo's privacy policies for details.

11. Changes to this policy

We will update this policy when our data practices change. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version. Where feasible, material changes will be announced in the app and/or by email at least 14 days before they take effect. Continued use of Saura after a change indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
